WARDROBE ORGANISING

Your clothes are cared for

It is easy to feel overwhelmed as we have accumulated to our wardrobe year after year. I relief that stress for you by helping you to decide what to keep, what to mend and what to donate. I will make your closet lighter, brighter and totally refreshed. Transforming your wardrobe space into something functional and beautiful. Cleaning out, decluttering, categorising your clothes by season, function and colour. Using the KonMari method of folding, providing tips and unique methods which will help you to get and to remain organised.
